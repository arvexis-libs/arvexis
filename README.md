# 🏦 Arvexis Core  
*Institutional Digital Asset Infrastructure — Custody, Strategy & Compliance, Unified*

Arvexis Core is the production-grade, regulatory-compliant platform powering institutional on-chain operations. Designed exclusively for licensed funds, family offices, and asset managers with ≥$10M digital AUM, it delivers end-to-end infrastructure for custody, programmable wallets, cross-protocol DeFi execution, and real-time portfolio risk analytics — all governed by financial-grade SLAs and audit-ready workflows.

## 🔑 Key Capabilities  

- **Regulated Qualified Custody**  
  MPC-based cold custody, licensed & insured in Singapore (MAS), BVI, and Cayman Islands. Supports segregated fund-level accounts, multi-sig + hardware key confirmation (YubiKey/Ledger), and LP-auditable custody reports.

- **Programmable Wallet Orchestration**  
  Deploy and govern purpose-built on-chain wallets: vaults, yield strategies, rebalancing bots, and settlement accounts — all with granular RBAC, time-locked approvals, and chain-agnostic policy enforcement.

- **DeFi Routing Engine**  
  Smart order routing across 15+ protocols (Uniswap v3, Curve, Aave, Pendle, etc.) with slippage-aware execution, MEV protection, and compliance guardrails (e.g., blacklist checks, counterparty scoring).

- **Real-Time Risk & Portfolio Dashboard**  
  Unified view of on-chain positions (spot, LP, derivatives, staking), PnL attribution, liquidity heatmaps, counterparty exposure, and VaR/ES modeling — synced with your OMS (via FIX/REST) and accounting systems (e.g., BlackLine, NetSuite).

## 📜 Compliance & Operations

- Audit logs retained for 7+ years; exportable for external auditors
