MPC architecture

Threshold signing: keys never exist whole

Our 3-of-5 design means no single party—including our own operators—can sign alone. Signing is coordinated across geographically separated HSM clusters, with cold vs. hot allocation tuned to your fund docs.

  • HSM-backed nodes; key material stays inside hardware boundaries
  • Threshold signatures end-to-end; full private keys are not reassembled in memory
  • Multi-region active-active; RTO/RPO targets can be written into the SLA
  • Air-gapped cold vaults with biometric access—physical and logical isolation
  • Live anomaly detection and circuit breakers; pause a chain or trade type by policy
